Costa Rica is well known for its beautiful plants and animals, terrific weather, and its kind and relaxed people. Costa Rica is often thought of synonymously with vacation, relaxation and health and in fact Costa Rica is home to one of only 5 Blue Zones on the planet. Blue Zones are locations where people live the longest, enjoy life the most and are healthiest. It’s for these reasons that Guanacaste, Costa Rica was the perfect location for the Rythmia Life Advancement Center.
