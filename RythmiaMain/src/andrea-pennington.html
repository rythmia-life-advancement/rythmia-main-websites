Meet Andrea Pennington

Dr. Andrea Pennington is a cis-gender female Black American integrative physician, meditation teacher, and psychedelic integration coach. She is also a bestselling author, international speaker, and founder of the online holistic health platform, In8Vitality, which integrates ancient wisdom with modern science and media.

With over two decades of medical practice specializing in trauma recovery, addiction medicine, traditional Chinese Medicine, and acupuncture, Dr. Andrea has provided medical services, workshops, and retreats to help people build resilience, reclaim vitality after burnout, recover from Adverse Childhood Experiences and nurture real self-love in order to thrive in all areas of life.

As a sought-after media personality for over twenty years, Dr. Andrea has shared her empowering wellness advice on the Oprah Winfrey Show, the Dr. Oz Show, iTV This Morning, CNN, The Today Show, LUXE-TV, Thrive Global, the HuffingtonPost, and as a news anchor for Discovery Health Channel.
