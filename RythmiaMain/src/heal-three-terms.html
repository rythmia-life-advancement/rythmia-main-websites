Heal Three Terms


  • The start date is on the day you purchase the R-Life aftercare package or rebook your next stay at Rythmia.
  • You have 60 days to recruit three guests from the time you enter the Heal Three Program. This must be the first trip to Rythmia for all three of your referrals.
  • All three guests must say they were referred by you at the time of booking and pay in full within 60 days of the start of your referral program.
  • Once all three final payments are made, you are eligible to book your FREE 7-night stay. You have up to one year to book and use this free trip. Example: Final payment for your third guest is made on December 14, 2022, you have up to December 14, 2023, to book and use your FREE 7-night stay.
  • Blackout Dates: The free stay cannot be redeemed on weeks when Rev. Michael Beckwith and/or Taita Juanito is on-site.
  • The Send Three, Heal Free Referral program can not be combined with Group Discounted rates.
  • Guest receives FREE 7-night stay at Rythmia in a Shared Double Queen Room w/ Free Round Trip Shuttle Service from Liberia International Airport, & no plant medicine fee.
