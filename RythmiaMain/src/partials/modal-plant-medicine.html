Plant Medicine Program


Before you Arrive

If you are participating in the Herbal Support Therapy program, there are some things we need you to understand before your arrival. There are two one-time fees that are due and payable at the resort. The first is a $400 fee for the licensed medical staff and personnel. This fee can be paid by using a credit or debit card. In addition, there is a $60 fee for plant medicine, which needs to be paid in cash to the medicine operating company on-site. You cannot use a credit or debit card for this charge.

Things We Need From You

Please send the name and phone number of an emergency contact.
