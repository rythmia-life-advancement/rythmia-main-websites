It is mandatory that we understand your mode of transportation to the resort as well as expected arrival time.   1. If you would like to take full advantage of the round-trip shuttle service from Liberia International airport, please provide us with your air travel itinerary so we are clear on your airline, flight number, and arrival date and time.

  2. If you would like to be picked up from the Hilton Garden Inn, the standard time for pickup is 11 AM.

  3. If you are driving directly to the resort or taking a cab, we will still need to understand your anticipated date and time of arrival at the resort so we can advise security at the gate.
