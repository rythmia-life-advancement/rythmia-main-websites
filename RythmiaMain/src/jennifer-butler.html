Meet Jennifer Butler

Jennifer Butler, founder of the True Beauty movement, has helped more than 7,000 clients find their authentic self-expression. Her philosophy of beauty as a spiritual principle has empowered a community of more than 100,000 online followers to tap into the secret power of their colors and design.

She has presented to leading companies including Nestle, Fox, and Google, and has been featured in Vogue, Glamour, the Los Angeles Times, and LA Weekly, and on CBS.

Jennifer holds a degree in sociology from Wilson College and is a graduate of the University of Santa Monica, where she studied spiritual psychology.

Learn more at www.jenniferbutlercolor.com
