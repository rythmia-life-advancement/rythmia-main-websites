Meet Brandee Sabella

Brandee Sabella is an impact-driven entrepreneur. She is the Co-Creator of the 9th Ray a healing center for artists. She produces and hosts transformational content, including movies, TV shows, and Podcasts.

Brandee founded the MEWE Club, a mastermind group of conscious entrepreneurs, disruptors, artists, and influencers engaged in mission-driven businesses and projects to heal and transform the world. Through the MEWE foundation, Brandee and her husband Damien support philanthropic projects protecting indigenous tribes, the Sacred Sites, and building orphanages around the globe.
