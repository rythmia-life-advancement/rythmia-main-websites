Ivan and Lana Rados

Meet Ivan and Lana Rados

Ivan and Lana have spent 28 years sharing their lives, loving each other, and overcoming life’s obstacles while learning from them. After working all this time in parallel, each with their own clients and in their own unique ways, the two healers have finally decided to embark on the next journey and combine their talents to life their healing powers to a truly cosmic level!

Together, they have created 1, 3, and 5 day workshop for individuals, couples, and groups. Participants in these workshops are guided, both on a conscious and unconscious level, by these two master healers.
