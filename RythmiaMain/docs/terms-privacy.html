The Award Process

Applications are reviewed based on financial eligibility as well as your interview with the scholarship administrator. Financial Eligibility is based on applicants making less than 400% of the 2019 Federal Poverty Guidelines 

Qualified applicants will be contacted by the scholarship administrator to verify income and to conduct a one on one interview. Applicants must provide proof of income.

Finalists will be notified via email and must reply to accept their award. Applicants that do not respond within 14 days forfeit their reward. 

The scholarship award covers all expenses at Rythmia LAC, Medicine Fees, and Transportation to and from Liberia Airport. Scholarship recipients are responsible for their own airfare to Rythmia and for any additional expenses.
