Foods to AVOID while partaking in Herbal Support Sessions
  • Coffee and Black Tea

Guests on the Herbal Support Therapy Program are encouraged to limit the amount of time they spend on the Internet and watching TV. Rather, fill your mind with positive and uplifting/spiritual messages and images to better sustain you through your healing journey.

Frequently Asked Questions


If you are participating in the Herbal Support Therapy program, there is a $160 ceremony fee, payable in cash when you arrive on-site. In addition, having some additional cash on hand is recommended should you wish to purchase additional services, gift shop amenities, or tip staff during your stay. US Dollars and Costa Rican Colones are accepted everywhere on and off site.

There is no alcohol on site and guests are not encouraged to drink alcohol during their stay. Furthermore, guests who are in the alternative medicine program, will not be allowed to participate in the alternative therapy sessions for the three days following any consumption of alcohol. Rythmia is a licensed medical facility, and any guest who is noticeably intoxicated, will be evaluated by the medical staff and is subject to be discharged without refund. There is a low tolerance for alcohol.

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. Any guest arriving to the resort after 5:00 PM may be required to delay their intake assessment until the morning of the following day. The intake assessment requires a minimum of 2 hours to complete, in order to prevent any interruption in your day one program schedule please arrive by 3:00 PM if possible..

We have doctors for two reasons. First and foremost, we want each guest to have medical support for general health issues; falls, cuts, colds, flus, etc… Further, we are a licensed medical facility that requires us to have licensed doctors on staff. All of this is for the sole benefit of our guests.

The Answer is You program was designed by Rev Michael Beckwith and is based off his the book. He’s hand selected trusted practitioners from the Agape International Spiritual Center to conduct the courses.

Children under the age of 18 years old are not allowed to spend the night at Rythmia. However, they can visit 7 days a week during the visiting hours of 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 8:00PM.

Rythmia needs a license to provide the holistic treatments and alternative medicine therapies we offer. Since we are a wellness center with the ability to handle medical and health-related issues for our guests, we need a license to operate as a healthcare facility in Costa Rica.

Rythmia is licensed by the Costa Rican Ministry of Health. We have a General Medical License to provide all the services we offer. We are also licensed by an additional department within the Ministry of Health called IAFA, which is the Costa Rican drug and alcohol facility management division. We are legal to operate within the municipality of Santa Cruz, Guanacaste.

Our medicine is made from ingredients ethically sourced within Costa Rica. We work with a healing combination of plants such as Banisteriopsis Caapi, Una De Gato, Mimosa Tenuiflora, and Nicotiana Rustica.
