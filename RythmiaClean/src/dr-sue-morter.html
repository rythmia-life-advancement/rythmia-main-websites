Meet Dr. Sue Morter

Internationally sought thought leader and transformational speaker, Master Teacher of Bio-Energetic Medicine and Quantum Field visionary, Dr. Sue Morter bridges the worlds of science and spirit through the embodiment of Unconditional Love, and reveals our deep, inherent Joy.

Dr. Sue is an expert on bridging science, spirit and human possibility and is seen in many documentary films including The Opus, The Cure is YOU, The Gift, and Le Femme. She has collaborated in programs with Wayne Dyer, Michael Beckwith, Marianne Williamson, Don Miguel Ruiz, Dr. John Gray, Dr. Bruce Lipton, and many other visionary leaders enlightening our conscious community today. She establishes our identity as creators of our complete life experience.

Through her Energy Codes® seminars and retreats, and her BodyAwake™ video series, she illuminatesthe relationships of quantum thoughts, energy medicine and healing through a heart-based process she teaches called Embodiment. She retrains the brain, shifts old memory patterns and then teaches us how to move beyond the brain to access our True and Essential Selves, based in Love.

She holds self- healing, yoga, and meditation retreats at sacred sites around the world in beautiful destinations like: Bali, Mexico, Peru, Egypt, Israel, and India, as well as many courses across the United States.

She is the founder of Morter Institute, an organization committed to teaching individuals self-healing techniques and a new approach to life, love and health based on Quantum Science and Energy Medicine. The Morter Institute is an international destination for healing and wellness education, providing ongoing coursework and treatment for tens of thousands of people over the last 30 years.

She also, along with her partner Dr. Elisa Zinberg, co-founded Soulful Science Productions, a production company focused on providing support for autistic and differently-abled children through guided meditation and affirmations.

Through an East meets West approach to the energy of well-being, Dr. Sue will reveal the deep connection between learning to still your thinking mind and train your brain to liberate your heart and embody your true Spirit Self. Healing and Empowerment are by-products of revealing our true and magnificent nature. Cutting edge Quantum Science, Energy Medicine and Heart Based Consciousness come together in this powerful and inspired invitation into creating a dimensional shift in how we perceive reality and our ability to heal.

Dr. Sue brings this information forward at this right time, in a user friendly, mind bending, and fun packed presentation sure to change your world.
