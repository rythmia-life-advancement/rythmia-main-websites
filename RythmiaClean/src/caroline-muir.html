Meet Caroline Muir

Caroline Muir is internationally celebrated as a Tantra Yoga educator, best-selling author, and the Queen of the modern Tantra movement.

Author of two best-selling books, Tantra Goddess, A Memoir of Sexual Awakening and Tantra, The Art of Conscious Loving, Caroline is the Co-Founder of The Sacred Feminine Mystery School.

Her teachings have impacted hundreds of thousands of students who appreciate the depth, integrity, and warmth she brings to this sensitive and ancient subject. Caroline has been featured in The Wall Street Journal, Yoga Journal, PlayGirl, Elle, Glamour, Esquire, Healthy Living, Asia Spa, and many others.

She continues to offer her teachings on rare occasions and facilitate private 3-day Immersions in Panama City, Panama. Caroline’s books will give you valuable background material for understanding Tantra Yoga and the Awakening Feminine.
