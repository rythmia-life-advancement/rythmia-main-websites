Meet Ben Decker

Ben Decker is a world-renowned meditation teacher, activist, and the best-selling author of Modern Spirituality: A Guide to the Heart of Mindfulness, Meditation, and the Art of Healing, as well as three other international bestselling books on meditation.

Ben is the host of The Modern Spirituality Podcast. His workshops are designed to help maximize your stay at Rythmia based on the tools and techniques he himself finds so beneficial with an emphasis on preparation beforehand, present moment awareness, and proactive integration strategies.

ELEMENTAL RHYTHM BREATHWORK EXPERIENCE

When we think of the word "initiation," we often think about the secret societies, the clubs, fraternities and sororities, and mystery school orders that have their initiation rituals. However, life itself has its own natural initiatory process.

Read More

These initiations are characterized by the learning that takes place as life unfolds, like having our first sexual experience, having our heart broken for the first time, or losing a loved one. These initiations leave us forever changed, and through the wisdom, your journey at Rythmia becomes a powerful initiation, leaving us forever changed for the better.

Embrace the depth and power of your medicine journey by consciously activating the universal technology of initiation. This workshop will support you in the intention-setting process, as well as integrating the visions, wisdom, and insight that comes through. Become deeply present through meditation-based exercises and develop an open-ended strategy for bringing shamanic wisdom home with you into your daily life.

Bring with you all of your honesty, all of your willingness, and all of the challenges that have held you back from your highest and fullest expression of yourself, and we will place them on the altar to be altered. Enter into this initiation with a pure heart and conscious intent, and receive the guidance, healing, and inspiration you need to be of greatest service to the collective. This week will be one that you will never forget.
