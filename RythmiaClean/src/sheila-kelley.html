Meet Sheila Kelley

Sheila Kelley is a thought leader and creator of the Sensual Feminine Movement phenomenon. She founded Sheila Kelley S Factor, a company dedicated to teaching feminine body language and pole dance in 2000 and has been teaching the movement and serving as CEO ever since.

S Factor is now an international movement with studios across the U.S. and retreats around the world. She also holds relationship workshops for couples and works with masculine energy and its healthy relationship to the feminine.

Sheila is a hands-on feminist committed to unearthing the erotic feminine power in our bodies and bringing that power back into the world. In her years of practice, she has elevated individuals and massive groups of women by teaching them to discover, heal and fully express their erotic feminine essence through movement.

Sheila has appeared on Oprah, The Ellen Degeneres Show, Late Night with Conan O’Brien, The Martha Stewart Show, The Tonight Show with Jay Leno, 20/20, 48 Hours and The View. Sheila works with notable thought leaders like Tony Robbins and Regena Thomashauer and is widely regarded as an authority on feminine empowerment.
