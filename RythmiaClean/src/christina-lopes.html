Meet Christina Lopes

Dr. Christina Lopes, DPT, MPH is a trained clinician and spiritual teacher who spent a decade working as a neuropediatric physical therapist before becoming a life coach in 2014. Today, she seamlessly bridges science and spirituality to help others heal from significant trauma, open their hearts, and live fulfilling lives.

Dr. Lopes’s 17-year career spans physical therapy, exercise science, mindfulness, energy medicine, life coaching, neuroscience, and spirituality. She has worked with over 2,000 private clients through her online coaching programs and private retreats and continues to reach hundreds of thousands more through her popular YouTube Channel (with over 25 million views).

Dr. Lopes holds a Doctorate Degree in Physical Therapy from New York University and a Master’s Degree in Public Health from Johns Hopkins University

SHADOW WORK 101: HOW TO EFFECTIVELY FACE YOUR INNER DEMONS & HEAL YOUR PAIN

Learn what your “shadow” actually is, how it negatively affects all areas of your life, and some powerful tips to help you do shadow work effectively.

On the journey of awakening, one must eventually come face to face with the aspects of ourselves that are hidden and buried deep within us. Without this conscious awareness, we cannot truly heal and release our past in order to move into a happier future. But for so many of us, confronting our “demons” feels scary and impossible, as if our pain were somehow stronger than our ability to heal.

In this workshop, you’ll learn how to become aware of what’s known as “your shadow” and how to heal it in a spiritually mature way, without bypassing. You’ll also discover to accelerate your shadow work and how to effectively use your time at Rythmia as a catalyst for healing your shadow.
