Meet Iyanla Vanzant

SFrom welfare mother to six-time New York Times best-selling author, from the Brooklyn projects to a multiple NAACP Image Award winner, from broken pieces to peace, Iyanla Vanzant is one of the country’s most prolific writers, public speakers, and among the most influential, socially engaged, spiritual life coaches of our time. Executive Producer and Host of the critically acclaimed Iyanla: Fix My Life on OWN: Oprah Winfrey Network. Host of one of the most talked about podcasts The R Spot with Shondaland Audio and iHeart.

Iyanla Vanzant’s focus on faith, empowerment, and loving relationships has inspired millions around the world.
