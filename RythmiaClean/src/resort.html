The Resort

Convenient yet secluded tropical location in beautiful Guanacaste, Costa Rica

Located in one of the five Blue Zones of the world, where people live happier, longer lives.

We’re in the gated community of Hacienda Pinilla. Beautiful Langosta Beach is nearby and shuttled excursions are included with your stay. Downtown Tamarindo is a 10-minute cab ride.

Fly into Liberia airport (airport code LIR). There are direct flights from a number of major cities in the U.S., such as Los Angeles (LAX) and New York (JFK). The resort is a 1.5 hour ride from the airport and shuttle rides are available.

Costa Rica is well known for its beautiful plant and animal life, terrific weather, and its kind and relaxed people. A haven for those seeking respite and relaxation, Costa Rica is home to one of only five blue zones on the planet.

Blue zones are locations where people live the longest, enjoy life the most, and are healthiest. It's for these reasons that Guanacaste, Costa Rica was the perfect location for Rythmia.
