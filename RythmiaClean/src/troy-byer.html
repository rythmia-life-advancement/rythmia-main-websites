Meet Troy Byer

Troy Byer is the founder of Return2U, an intentional transformational organization designed to Ignite, Elevate and Inspire. She’s also a best-selling author that has been leading transformational seminars for 15 years.

Earning a master’s degree in Community Psychology from Pacifica Graduate Institute, she’s also currently earning a doctorate in Clinical Psychology from California Southern University.

Ms. Byer is a certified HeartMath© Trainer, and an active member of The Agape Spiritual Center, and Single Moms Planet.
