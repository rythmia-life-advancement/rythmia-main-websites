Meet Sherrill Sellman

Sherrill Sellman, N.D., Naturopathic Doctor (Board Certified in Integrative Medicine), is an educator, womens natural health expert, psychotherapist and journalist in the field of womens health. She is a passionate advocate for regaining hormonal health, naturally, for women of all ages.

Her programs also nutrigenomics for optimal aging, keys for rejuvenating the body and learning the code for getting younger and healthier at any age. Dr. Sherrill is committed to exploring the leading edge solutions for personal and global transformation.

As an explorer of the deepest sources of personal and spiritual transformation, Dr. Sherrill has studied with Native American elders, Australian Aboriginal elders, Hawaiian elders.

She has also ventured in the Amazon to experience the wisdom of Ancient wisdom with Shipibo shaman. Her profound healing from the Ancient wisdom ceremonies led her to Rythmia Life Advancement Center, where she has continued her unfolding spiritual journey.

Dr. Sherrill has an international Naturopathic Medicine practice. She is also, a much sought after keynote presenter and international lecturer. For the past 12 years, she has been the host of What Women MUST Know, a weekly internet radio show. Dr. Sellman is also a contributing writer to numerous health publications worldwide.

She is the best-selling author of Hormone Heresy: What Women MUST Know, What Women MUST Know to Protect Their Daughters from Breast Cancer and Chia: The Return of the Ancient Seed. Her latest book is Hemp Health Revolution: The A-Z Health Benefits of Hemp Extract.

Her professional credentials and commitment to an integrative healing approach extends over a 30-year period.

She can be found at whatwomenmustknow.com and FB/whatwomenmustknow.
